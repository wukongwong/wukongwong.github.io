    IEEE/ASME Transactions on Mechatronics, 2022
    SCI Q1
    Abs DOI Bib HTML Video

    This paper presents a learning-based approach to enable adaptive grasping from human demonstrations. We collect human teleoperation data and use supervised learning to train neural networks that can handle object variability in size and stiffness.
